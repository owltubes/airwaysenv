AirWays Environmental Services
Introduction
Indoor Air Quality Testing & Consulting
AirWays Environmental Services is an indoor environmental health consulting practice serving the New Jersey and New York City area. Based in Red Bank, NJ, AirWays was founded in 1992 by Steven Temes, Industrial Hygienist and ACAC Certified Microbial Consultant.
Professional Services
• Investigations of building-related health effects in commercial buildings and homes

• Mold assessments

• Mold post-remediation verification (PRV) inspections and "clearance" testing

• Odor investigations

• Commercial building indoor air quality (IAQ) surveys

• Healthy home inspections

• Air sampling for identification and measurement of indoor contaminants
